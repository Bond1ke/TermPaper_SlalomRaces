Term paper - "Game made with SDL". Game - "Slalom/races".
Made by Bogdan Gulmanov, 1st year student of the VOENMEH (group O714b).

You can navigate through the main menu and pause menu by 
pressing arrow keys on your keyboard or by using your mouse.

The goal of the game is to collect as many points as possible in less time. 
Player gets 100 ponts if he passes between the flags.

To control the player use left and right arrow keys on your keyboard.
